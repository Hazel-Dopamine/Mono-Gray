The Story of Us

We decided to name our company Hazel Dopamine. Given name is to make our customers and consumer’s to be happy and bright during their visit. Moreover, the purpose of our café is to make our client addicted to be merry drinking our coffee. The logo that we created represented the color of our signature menu, Hazelnut Coffee. The circle in the logo indicates the unity our ingredient working together to achieve the perfect cup of coffee.




Hazel Dopamine is an elegant themed cafe with hazelnuts. Our cafe serves many types of drink flavors such as coffee, tea and chocolate. Each drink has its own mix and unique taste. Apart from that, our cafe also serves desserts as a side dish while our customers are hanging out in our cafe. With that said, it is not a problem for customers to take a long time to hang out in our cafe as we also serve desserts for dessert while hanging out. Other than that, this is because the desserts and types of drinks we serve are a compatible combination and are able to give customers peace of mind.
